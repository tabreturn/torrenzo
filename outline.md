# SLOs

- **a** Identify and describe tropical fruits using sensory vocabulary like aroma, sweetness, and texture. ^slo-a
- **b** Sketch fruit compositions with simple labels and captions to record seasonal availability. ^slo-b
- **c** Organize a digital collection of fruit images and notes, applying a consistent tagging scheme for easy reference. ^slo-c
- **d** Share a brief write-up that compares two different fruits and what makes each one unique in presentation and flavor. ^slo-d
