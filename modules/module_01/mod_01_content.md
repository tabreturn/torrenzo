## Overview

Welcome to the playful first module where we learn to observe colour, texture, and aroma as if we were curating a tiny fruit museum. Keep your notes short and positive—there are no wrong answers, only interesting discoveries.

## Key Questions

- What does each fruit remind you of when you touch it? 
- How would you describe the texture to someone who cannot see it? 
- Which colours pop first, and how do they change depending on light or context?

## Tools and Techniques

1. Choose a notebook or digital document to record a list of adjectives.
2. Sketch each fruit quickly, capturing distinctive curves and edges.
3. Pair each sketch with 2–3 words that highlight the most memorable sensory detail.

Use this module to build a comfortable routine: see, note, and celebrate the small details.
