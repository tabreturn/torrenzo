## Activity 1: Orchard Interview

Talk with someone at home or in your community about a fruit they often enjoy. Ask them to describe how it smells and tastes, and what memory or moment is tied to that fruit.

## Activity 2: Market Moodboard

Collect a few colours, words, or small sketches that capture the atmosphere of a fruit market. Arrange them in a collage-style layout on paper or in a digital doc, then explain what mood it conveys.

## Activity 3: Context Capsule

Write a short note (three sentences) that describes where you imagine a fruit journey might begin and end. Focus on people, places, and small rituals that make it special.
