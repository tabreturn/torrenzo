# Module 02: Fruit Stories in Context

{{ subject_learning_outcomes }}

## Overview

In Module 2 we stretch beyond observation to consider where fruits come from, who enjoys them, and how they appear in markets and celebrations. This module blends curiosity, simple research, and gentle reflection.

## Focus Areas

- trace the journey of a fruit from orchard to table
- notice how people describe the same fruit differently
- lean into narratives that celebrate seasons, rituals, or everyday moments

## Workspace Prompts

1. Pick a fruit you love. Write a short paragraph about who introduced you to that fruit or where you first tasted it.
2. Ask someone else (friend, family, or a classmate) how they would describe the same fruit; note the differences.
3. Collect one image or description from the web, a book, or a magazine that shows the fruit being enjoyed formally or casually. Explain why it stood out.

Let this module help you see context, culture, and story, not just colours and flavours.
